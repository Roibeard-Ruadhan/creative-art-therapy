Creative Art Therapy Sessions

WHAT IT IS?

Art therapy is a branch of psychotherapy. It uses spontaneous, creative expression within a therapeutic relationship to assist in maintaining a good mental health. No experience with art materials necessary. All ages welcome.


Art therapists are master-level clinicians trained to draw out the inner feelings and emotions of a client, in a safe and non-judgmental manner.

“I could say things with color and shape that I couldn’t say any other way – things I had no words for…” – Margaret Naumburg

WHO CAN BENEFIT?

Everyone has a creative side, although it may never have been formally recognised, you need no experience or skill to benefit from art therapy.


Any age can benefit from art therapy and being creative via the art making process.

“It is only in being creative that an individual discovers the self” D.W. Winnicott

BENEFITS FOR ADULTS:

  • • Greater self-awareness
  • • Greater self-esteem
  • • Increased coping skills
  • • Lowered stress and anxiety
  • • A feeling of empowerment
  • • Improved Emotional stability

“Art is my cure to all this madness, sadness and loss of belonging in the world & through it I’ll walk myself home.” ― Nikki Rowe

BENIFITS FOR ADOLESCENTS & CHILDREN:

  • • Can strengthen the Child/Adolescents inner support structure
  • • Can help develop good contact skills
  • • Can help develop a better sense of self
  • • Can help define the sense of self

What Kate offers:

Kate offers weekly sessions which last 60 minutes in her private practice office in Glasnevin and Lucan.


She facilitates each client individually and attends fortnightly supervision to help her give her clients the best experience.


She uses certain tried and tested methods and intervenes when needed. No experience with art materials necessary.
